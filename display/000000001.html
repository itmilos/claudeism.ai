Independent Signatories of
The Manifesto for Claudeism

We are uncovering better ways of thinking with AI by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work we have come to value:
  • Craft over commands
  • Dialogue over directives
  • Iteration over perfection
  • Understanding over mere output

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.
